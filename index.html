Parlor Palm Care Guide

Soil for Parlor Palm

Well-draining potting mix.

Parlor Palms thrive in well-draining soil with organic matter, providing a balanced environment for root health.

Watering

Moderate, consistent watering.

Keep the soil consistently moist for Parlor Palms, allowing the top inch to dry out between watering sessions.

Lighting

Indirect, bright light.

Place Parlor Palms in locations with indirect, bright light; they can tolerate lower light conditions but prefer bright, filtered light.

Benefits

Air-purifying qualities.

Parlor Palms help improve indoor air quality by removing pollutants and adding oxygen to the environment.

Low maintenance.

These palms are relatively easy to care for, making them a great choice for indoor greenery with minimal effort.

Non-Benefits

Avoid overwatering; excessive moisture can lead to root rot. Watch for pests like spider mites.
